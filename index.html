Avenue Supermarts Limited, d/b/a DMart, is an Indian retail corporation that operates a chain of hypermarkets in India. It was founded by Radhakishan Damani in 2002 when its first store was opened in Powai, Mumbai. As of June 2023, it has 330 stores across 14 states in India.
